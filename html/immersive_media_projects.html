Forest Adventure

Project Background

Physics is an abstract subject with many complex equations that can often be difficult to understand. To help address this problem, Forest Adventure provides an engaging environment for students to learn about Newtonian mechanics and the kinematic equations of motion. As a proof of concept, Forest Adventure shows how game-based learning can help students learn the use of these equations in a fun and visual way.

Designed as a multi-level platformer game, Forest Adventure challenges students to use the equations of motions to progress from one level to the next. For example, students must calculate the correct final velocities from different speed boosts to defeat their enemies.

Key Benefits

  • Puzzle based reinforcement of complex physics concepts for more engaging learning.
  • The enhancement of student motivation through the application of gamified learning techniques.

Key Technologies Used

  • Unity Game Engine
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • MAYA Autodesk

Potential Enhancements

This application can be enhanced with the following:

  • Implementations of more physics formulas
  • Sound effects
  • Vfx and lighting design
  • Implementations of more level for each formula

Kidney Defense

Project Background

Taking care of your kidney early is important because the effects of kidney disease are unreversible. Hence, this project was created to spread awareness about the importance of kidney health and as the many of the younger generations do not take care of their kidneys and do not know the risks of harming their kidney.

Kidney defense is a game that is playable on the web where the player defend your kidney against enemy fast food characters using ally sports characters. It teaches the player how eating too much fast food can harm your kidney and doing sports can help protect your kidney.

Key Benefits

  • Awareness – Spread awareness about the importance of kidney health to the younger generations
  • Educate – Teach the younger generations on how to take care of their kidney by doing sports

Key Technologies Used

  • AutoDesk Maya
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe XD
  • Unity

Potential Enhancements

This application can be enhanced with the following:

  • Adding more enemy types to the game
  • Adding more ally characters representing more sports
  • Using a more complex model for object detection.

Get Fit!

Project Background

Students today are living more sedentary lives, with the advent of home-based learning and work from home lifestyles, students have less and less of an incentive to go outside and exercise, which can lead to health problems that can manifest in the later stages of their lives.

This projects aims to solve this issue, by incentivizing students to engage in more active lifestyles through the form of a mobile app. Even a simple walk around their neighborhood or jog can do wonders for their physical health. The app also features a meal tracking feature, and integration with other apps so students can more accurately monitor their own health and plan their routes in advance.

This app makes use of built-in sensors that come with modern Android devices to track a user’s steps. Firebase to securely log in, register, and store user data, and QR Codes to help students share their own meal information to other students.

Key Benefits

  • Health Awareness – Incentivizes students to live healthier lifestyles by rewarding their efforts to participate in physical activities.
  • Convenience – Helps students keep track of what they put into their bodies through exercise and meal tracking.

Key Technologies Used

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Android Studio

Potential Enhancements

This application can be enhanced with the following:

  • A machine learning model that detects if a user is running, walking, jogging etc.
  • More statistics when tracking user steps

The Mini Escape

Project Background

The Mini Escape is an online gaming hub in Roblox for players of all ages and types to converge and play together. It is a casual game where players can unwind and play leisurely. Players are insects who are accidently getting baked in the kitchen oven. They have to overcome many obstacles and race against time to successfully escape from the house. This game encourages competition and interaction with other players; jumping from platform to platform and seeing who can finish a level the fastest and win prizes.

When the COVID-19 pandemic happened, physical/social interactivity was reduced to prevent the spread of the virus. Many were mentally affected by the virus as they stayed at home for months. The usage of Roblox changed that allowed players to connect with others through the digital world rather than the physical world. In the past few months, Hyundai, Vans, Nike and many more big brands created experiences in Roblox to create a social experience for the players and for marketing purposes.

Key Benefits

  • Sociability – Allows for people of all ages and variety to interact and play together. Converse, interact and befriend many new people online through play thus making it an enriching gaming experience​
  • Convenient – Allow people of all ages to converge and play together in a light hearted manner that is accessible in Roblox, a free to play platform.​

Key Technologies Used

  • Roblox Studio
  • Maya and Blender
  • Adobe Photoshop

Potential Enhancements

This project can be enhanced with the following:

  • Replayability
  • Mechanic Variety
