Automation of Data Extraction and Invoice Parking Using RPA

Project Background

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) refers to a software that can be easily programmed to do basic, repetitive tasks across applications. It creates and deploys a software robot with the ability to launch and operate other software and is designed primarily for office-type functions. RPA works like a digital assistant, doing routine onerous tasks that would otherwise eat up employees' time.

Problems Identified

Parking an invoice is the process of taking data from an invoice and typing it into a database. This process is very time-consuming and repetitive, wasting the time and effort of workers who have to manually complete the tasks.

Project Objective

To use a combination of Python and RPA to make a program that can automatically extract and collect data from invoices and park it into the company’s database.

Business Value

This project will help to increase the productivity and work efficiency of workers as they can focus more of their time on important tasks.

FiNDNet BI Report

Project Background

Business intelligence is the technology that allows businesses and organizations to organize their data, analyse them, as well as help to understand better on their business data.

In this pandemic period, the internal users in the Operational Team and Finance team, as well as external users from security companies are looking for the development of new reports by the BI team in Dnata using the source data from FiNDNet. Dnata is a company based in the air industry, the company is using FiNDnet Suite which is a comprehensive ground handling software solution developed by another company, Damarel systems.

The area we are looking at in this project for the FiNDNet system is the FiNDNet operational database, where flight information is inputted by a team handling ground handling operations in Dnata. For this project, we use multiple datasets from FiNDNet as our source data to develop BI reports after the transformation and cleaning of the data.

Objectives of the project

Retrieval of source data from FiNDNet and performing transformation & cleansing to get high quality data for development of reports and dashboards.

To develop reports and dashboards for different use cases so that internal and external users in the company can perform their daily reporting as well as to gain insights on data from the developed reports and dashboards.

Business Value

The business value for this project would be that there is a method to visualize clean data to make accurate data driven decisions after the development of reports and dashboards, which is a solution that BI is only able to achieve.

Furthermore, without clean and transformed data, as well as the development of reports and dashboards, internal and external users would have to refer to unclean source data without the right format for reporting and gaining insights. This would lead to inaccurate data driven decisions and would be detrimental to the company.

    Balefire V3

    Project Background

    From 2019 onwards, No Smoking Zone (ORNSZ) and Designated Smoking Areas (DSAs) were introduced in Singapore which started in Orchard Road precinct and progressively to other areas in Singapore. To automatically monitor the utilisation of these ORNSZs and DSAs by counting the number of smoking instances, Balefire was developed. Balefire is a smoking detection AI-model trained using deep learning techniques. It detects and returns the counts of the number of unique persons and smokers from a camera’s field-of-view. The AI model enabled the agency to obtain the counts for trending analysis without heavy manual manpower efforts. This project is to train and develop the 3rd iteration of the AI model where the key objective is to improve the count accuracies while maintaining the operating costs.

    Key Benefits

    Manpower savings - The smoking detection engine is a lot efficient in automatically counting the number of smoking instances from CCTVs/video feeds without needing a person to manually watch and count them in videos.

    Scalable - The solution is expandable according to the number of CCTVs/video feeds

    Key Technologies Used

    • Person Detection
    • Person Tracker
    • Pose Estimation
    • Face Classifier

    Potential Enhancements

    This project can be enhanced with the following:

  • Refinement of the model to further improve the accuracies
  • Implement new and more cutting AI models as part of the solution stack
