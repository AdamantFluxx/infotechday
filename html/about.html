About



The Temasek Polytechnic InfoTech Day is an annual event organised by the School of Informatics & IT. It comprises of seminars and the showcase of innovative projects by our final year students. This year, the theme is 'Building the future with IT'. The projects were developed in collaboration with industry partners and various government agencies employing the latest technologies in the key areas (AI & Analytics, Business Digitalisation, Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics and Immersive Media).
